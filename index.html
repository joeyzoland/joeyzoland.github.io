About Me

I am a San Jose native, and have lived in the Silicon Valley my entire life. I obtained my B.A. and M.A. degrees in psychology from San Jose State University. Whenever possible, I tailored my coursework towards educational psychology; I have always loved learning, and I feel very fortunate to now be in an industry where I get to learn something new each and every day.

After obtaining exposure to coding through Massive Open Online Courses, including EdX and Udacity, as well as utilizing python for data analysis in market research, I decided to transition into coding full-time. To this end, I enrolled in Coding Dojo's intense boot camp program, which includes 1000 hours of instruction for 3 full stacks of development within 14 weeks. After graduating with top honors in each stack, I joined a Scrum team that is currently designing a silent bidding auction website. Upon completion, this website will facilitate fundraising efforts for the Valley Medical Center Foundation.
